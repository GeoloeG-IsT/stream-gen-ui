# Utilities Department

The Utilities Department ensures reliable delivery of essential services including water, electricity, waste management, and sustainable energy initiatives. We maintain critical infrastructure and work toward Berlin's goal of becoming carbon-neutral by 2040.

## Water Services

### Klaus Weber
**Role:** Water Services Director
**Email:** klaus.weber@berlin-city.de
**Phone:** +49 30 2302 5100
**Office:** Utilities Building, 3rd Floor
**Hours:** Mon-Fri 7:00-15:00

Klaus oversees Berlin's water treatment, distribution, and wastewater management systems. He manages water quality testing, infrastructure maintenance, and emergency response for water main breaks. With a degree in environmental engineering and 18 years in water management, Klaus ensures Berlin's water meets all safety standards. He coordinates water conservation programs and manages the city's stormwater management initiatives. Contact Klaus for questions about water quality, billing disputes, to report water main breaks, or to request water quality test results for your neighborhood.
