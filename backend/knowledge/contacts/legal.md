# Legal Department

The Legal Department provides legal counsel to city government, represents Berlin in legal proceedings, manages public records, ensures regulatory compliance, and promotes transparency. We protect the city's legal interests while ensuring open and accountable government.

## Legal Counsel

**Role:** City Attorney
**Email:** heinrich.weber@berlin-city.de
**Phone:** +49 30 2307 1100
**Office:** City Hall, 7th Floor
**Hours:** Mon-Fri 9:00-17:00

Dr. Weber serves as Berlin's chief legal officer, providing legal advice to the City Council, Mayor, and all city departments. He oversees litigation, reviews contracts and ordinances, and ensures city operations comply with applicable laws. With over 25 years of municipal law experience, Dr. Weber handles complex legal matters including land use disputes, employment law, constitutional issues, and intergovernmental agreements.
