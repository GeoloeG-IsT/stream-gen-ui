# Health Department

The Health Department promotes and protects the health and well-being of all Berlin residents. We provide public health services, environmental health oversight, mental health programs, disease prevention, and health education initiatives.

## Public Health Administration

**Office:** Health Services Building, 5th Floor
**Hours:** Mon-Fri 9:00-17:00

Dr. Vogel leads Berlin's public health programs and oversees disease surveillance, outbreak investigation, and health policy development.
