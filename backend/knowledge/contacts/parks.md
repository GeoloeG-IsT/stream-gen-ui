# Parks and Recreation Department

The Parks and Recreation Department enhances quality of life by providing parks, recreational facilities, community programs, and natural resource conservation. We create opportunities for play, fitness, social connection, and appreciation of nature.

## Parks Administration

### Gabriele Schulz
**Role:** Parks Director
**Email:** gabriele.schulz@berlin-city.de
**Phone:** +49 30 2308 2100
**Office:** Parks Administration Building
**Hours:** Mon-Fri 8:00-16:00

Gabriele oversees Berlin's 45 parks, 12 playgrounds, and 200 hectares of green space. She manages park maintenance, landscape improvements, and urban forestry programs. With a degree in landscape architecture and 18 years in park management, Gabriele balances recreation needs with environmental conservation. She coordinates park master planning, manages the park improvement budget, and oversees staff training. Contact Gabriele for park maintenance issues, to propose park improvements, discuss park naming or dedications, or to provide input on park planning.

## Community Gardens and Nature Programs

### Linda Hoffmann
**Role:** Community Gardens Coordinator
**Email:** linda.hoffmann@berlin-city.de
**Phone:** +49 30 2308 2120
**Office:** Parks Administration Building
**Hours:** Mon-Fri 9:00-17:00

Linda manages Berlin's community garden program, overseeing 8 community gardens with 200+ plots. She coordinates plot assignments, provides gardening education, organizes volunteer workdays, and manages garden infrastructure. Linda promotes organic gardening practices and coordinates the seed library program. She works with schools to create educational gardens and manages the pollinator garden initiative. Contact Linda to apply for garden plots, get gardening advice, volunteer at community gardens, or start a new community garden in your neighborhood.
