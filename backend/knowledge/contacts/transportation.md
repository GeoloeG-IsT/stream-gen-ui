# Transportation Department

The Transportation Department manages Berlin's multimodal transportation network, including public transit coordination, street maintenance, bike infrastructure, parking services, and accessibility programs. We work to provide safe, efficient, and sustainable transportation options for all residents.

## Public Transit

### Petra Schneider
**Role:** Transit Services Coordinator
**Email:** petra.schneider@berlin-city.de
**Phone:** +49 30 2303 6100
**Office:** Transportation Center, 4th Floor
**Hours:** Mon-Fri 8:00-16:00

Petra serves as the liaison between the city and regional transit providers, coordinating bus and tram services within Berlin. She processes route change requests, manages the bus shelter maintenance program, and works on transit accessibility improvements. Petra analyzes ridership data to identify service gaps and advocates for schedule adjustments. She coordinates with city planning to ensure new developments have adequate transit access. ## Streets and Infrastructure

### Robert Zimmerman
**Role:** Street Maintenance Supervisor
**Email:** robert.zimmerman@berlin-city.de
**Phone:** +49 30 2303 6120
**Office:** Public Works Yard, Main Office
**Hours:** Mon-Fri 6:00-14:00, Emergency 24/7

Robert oversees maintenance and repair of Berlin's 450 kilometers of streets and roads. He manages pothole repair crews, coordinates street resurfacing projects, and ensures proper street signage. Robert leads the winter snow and ice removal operations and manages the street sweeping program. He responds to emergency situations like sinkholes or major pavement failures and coordinates street closures for events and construction. Contact Robert to report potholes, damaged street signs, drainage issues, or to request street condition assessments.

## Parking and Traffic

**Phone:** +49 30 2303 6130

**Hours:** Mon-Fri 8:00-16:00

Hans manages on-street and municipal parking facilities throughout Berlin. He oversees parking meter maintenance, permit programs, and parking enforcement coordination.
