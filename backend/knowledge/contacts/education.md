# Education and Cultural Services Department

The Education and Cultural Services Department supports lifelong learning, preserves cultural heritage, and provides access to information and educational opportunities. We manage library services, museums, cultural programs, and educational initiatives for all ages.

## Library Services

### Barbara Klein
**Role:** Library Director
**Email:** barbara.klein@berlin-city.de
**Phone:** +49 30 2309 3100
**Office:** Central Library, 2nd Floor
**Hours:** Mon-Fri 9:00-17:00

Barbara leads Berlin's public library system with one central library and three branch locations. She manages collection development, library programming, and strategic planning for library services. With a master's in library science and 15 years of experience, Barbara champions digital literacy, community engagement, and equitable access to information. She oversees the library's transformation into a community hub offering maker spaces, meeting rooms, and cultural programs. Contact Barbara for library policy questions, programming suggestions, to discuss library partnerships, or to provide feedback on library services.
