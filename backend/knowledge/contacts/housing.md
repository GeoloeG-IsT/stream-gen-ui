# Housing and Community Development Department

The Housing and Community Development Department works to ensure all Berlin residents have access to safe, affordable housing. We manage housing programs, tenant assistance, building inspections, homeless services, and community development initiatives.

## Affordable Housing

### Dr. Maria Schmidt
**Role:** Affordable Housing Director
**Email:** maria.schmidt@berlin-city.de
**Phone:** +49 30 2310 4100
**Office:** Housing Services Building, 4th Floor
**Hours:** Mon-Fri 9:00-17:00

Dr. Schmidt leads Berlin's affordable housing initiatives including housing assistance programs, development of affordable units, and preservation of existing affordable housing stock. She manages relationships with housing developers, administers housing subsidy programs, and coordinates with state and federal housing agencies. Maria develops policies to increase affordable housing supply and manages the inclusionary zoning program. She oversees the first-time homebuyer program and coordinates affordable housing advocacy. Contact Dr. Schmidt for information about affordable housing programs, development opportunities, or housing policy questions.

## Tenant Services

### Julia Becker
**Role:** Tenant Rights Coordinator
**Email:** julia.becker@berlin-city.de
**Phone:** +49 30 2310 4110
**Office:** Housing Services Building, 2nd Floor
**Hours:** Mon-Fri 9:00-17:00, Walk-in hours Mon/Wed 1:00-4:00

Julia provides information and assistance to tenants regarding their rights and responsibilities. She educates tenants about fair housing laws, mediates landlord-tenant disputes, and provides referrals to legal services. Julia coordinates the tenant education workshop series and manages the rental housing complaint program. She investigates housing discrimination complaints and works to resolve disputes before they escalate to legal proceedings. Contact Julia for tenant rights questions, to report housing discrimination, mediation assistance, or to schedule tenant rights workshops.
