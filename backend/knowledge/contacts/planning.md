# Planning and Development Department

The Planning and Development Department guides Berlin's physical growth and evolution, managing land use, zoning, building permits, historic preservation, and sustainable development initiatives. We balance growth with quality of life and environmental protection.

## Historic Preservation

**Email:** elisabeth.braun@berlin-city.de
**Phone:** +49 30 2305 8130
**Office:** Planning Department, 5th Floor
**Hours:** Mon-Fri 9:00-17:00

Elisabeth manages Berlin's historic preservation program, protecting buildings and districts with historical, architectural, or cultural significance. She reviews proposed changes to historic properties, manages the historic district designation process, and provides technical assistance for historic building rehabilitation. Elisabeth administers the historic preservation grant program and educates property owners about preservation best practices.
