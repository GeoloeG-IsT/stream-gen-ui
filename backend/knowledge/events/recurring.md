# Recurring Events

## Weekly Events

### City Council Work Sessions
**Schedule:** Every Tuesday at 16:00
**Location:** City Hall, Conference Room 301
**Department:** City Council
**Type:** Work Session

Informal working meetings of the City Council to discuss upcoming agenda items, receive staff presentations, and deliberate on policy matters. Open to the public but no public comment period. Regular Council meetings with public comment occur monthly.

**Contact:** City Clerk (clerk@berlin-city.de)

---

### Farmers Market
**Schedule:** Every Saturday, 08:00 - 13:00 (May through October)
**Location:** City Hall Plaza
**Department:** Economic Development
**Type:** Market

Weekly farmers market featuring local produce, baked goods, crafts, and prepared foods. Live music, activities for children, and seasonal events. Vendor applications available at berlin-city.de/farmers-market.

**Contact:** Thomas Richter (thomas.richter@berlin-city.de)

---

### Community Garden Open Hours
**Schedule:** Every Wednesday and Saturday, 09:00 - 12:00
**Location:** All 8 community gardens
**Department:** Parks and Recreation
**Type:** Open Hours

Community gardens open for plot holders and visitors. Garden coordinators available to answer questions. Tools available for use. Great time to meet fellow gardeners and learn tips.

**Contact:** Linda Hoffmann (linda.hoffmann@berlin-city.de)

---

### Story Time at the Library
**Schedule:** Every Tuesday and Thursday at 10:30
**Location:** Central Library, Children's Room
**Department:** Education and Cultural Services
**Type:** Children's Program

Story time for preschool-aged children (ages 3-5). Stories, songs, and activities to promote early literacy. Free and no registration required. Caregivers must accompany children.

**Contact:** Central Library (library@berlin-city.de)

---

### Senior Fitness Classes
**Schedule:** Every Monday, Wednesday, Friday at 09:00
**Location:** Community Center, Fitness Room
**Department:** Parks and Recreation
**Type:** Fitness Program

Low-impact fitness classes designed for seniors. Improve strength, flexibility, and balance. No registration required, drop-in welcome. First class free, then $3 per class or $25 for 10-class punch card.

**Contact:** Monika Fischer (monika.fischer@berlin-city.de)

---

## Biweekly Events

### Planning Commission Meetings
**Schedule:** First and third Tuesday of each month at 18:00
**Location:** City Hall, Main Chamber
**Department:** Planning
**Type:** Public Meeting

Regular meetings of the Planning Commission to review development applications, zoning requests, and planning policy. Public comment accepted on agenda items. Agendas posted 72 hours before meetings.

**Contact:** Andreas Neumann (andreas.neumann@berlin-city.de)

---

### Technology Help Desk at Library
**Schedule:** First and third Saturday of each month, 13:00 - 16:00
**Location:** Central Library, Technology Center
**Department:** Education and Cultural Services
**Type:** Assistance Program

Free one-on-one technology assistance. Bring your device for help with email, internet, apps, e-books, or basic troubleshooting. 30-minute appointments (walk-ins accepted if slots available).

**Contact:** Thomas Weber (thomas.weber@berlin-city.de)

---

## Monthly Events

### City Council Meetings
**Schedule:** First Wednesday of each month at 19:00
**Location:** City Hall, Council Chamber
**Department:** City Council
**Type:** Public Meeting

Regular monthly meetings of the Berlin City Council. Public comment period begins at 19:00 (sign up starts at 18:30). Full agendas available 48 hours before meetings at berlin-city.de/council.

**Contact:** City Clerk (clerk@berlin-city.de)

---

### Business Licensing Office Extended Hours
**Schedule:** Last Thursday of each month, 08:00 - 19:00
**Location:** Finance Building, 1st Floor
**Department:** Finance
**Type:** Extended Service Hours

Extended hours for business license applications and renewals. Convenient for business owners who can't visit during regular hours. All business licensing services available.

**Contact:** Martin Schroeder (martin.schroeder@berlin-city.de)

---

### Community Coffee with the Mayor
**Schedule:** Second Thursday of each month at 08:00
**Location:** Various locations (check monthly schedule)
**Department:** Mayor's Office
**Type:** Community Meeting

Informal gathering to meet the Mayor, ask questions, and discuss community issues. Rotates between different neighborhoods. Coffee and pastries provided. No registration required.

**Contact:** Mayor's Office (mayor@berlin-city.de)

---

### Historic Preservation Commission
**Schedule:** Third Thursday of each month at 18:00
**Location:** Planning Department, Conference Room
**Department:** Planning
**Type:** Public Meeting

Monthly meeting to review proposed changes to historic properties and districts. Public comment accepted on agenda items. Agendas available one week before meetings.

**Contact:** Elisabeth Braun (elisabeth.braun@berlin-city.de)

---

### Board of Health Meetings
**Schedule:** Fourth Monday of each month at 17:00
**Location:** Health Services Building, Conference Room
**Department:** Health
**Type:** Public Meeting

Monthly meeting of the Board of Health to discuss public health policy, review health programs, and address community health concerns. Public comment period included.

**Contact:** Dr. Christine Vogel (christine.vogel@berlin-city.de)

---

### Transportation Advisory Committee
**Schedule:** Second Tuesday of each month at 17:30
**Location:** Transportation Center, Conference Room
**Department:** Transportation
**Type:** Advisory Committee

Citizens advisory committee providing input on transportation planning, traffic issues, and transit service. Public welcome to attend and provide comment.

**Contact:** Petra Schneider (petra.schneider@berlin-city.de)

---

## Quarterly Events

### Hazardous Waste Collection Day
**Schedule:** Second Saturday of March, June, September, December; 08:00 - 14:00
**Location:** Sanitation Center
**Department:** Utilities
**Type:** Special Collection

Quarterly opportunity to safely dispose of household hazardous waste including paint, chemicals, batteries, electronics, and fluorescent bulbs. Free for residents. See website for complete list of accepted items.

**Contact:** Emma Fischer (emma.fischer@berlin-city.de)

---

### Emergency Preparedness Training
**Schedule:** Third Saturday of February, May, August, November; 09:00 - 16:00
**Location:** Fire Station 1, Training Facility
**Department:** Public Safety
**Type:** Training Program

Free Community Emergency Response Team (CERT) training. Learn disaster response skills including fire safety, light search and rescue, and medical operations. Advance registration required (limited to 30 participants per session).

**Contact:** Jennifer Nguyen (jennifer.nguyen@berlin-city.de)

---

### Economic Development Quarterly Report
**Schedule:** Last Tuesday of January, April, July, October at 12:00
**Location:** Finance Building, Auditorium (also livestreamed)
**Department:** Finance
**Type:** Public Presentation

Quarterly presentation on economic indicators, business development, employment trends, and development activity. Lunch provided for in-person attendees (RSVP required).

**Contact:** Thomas Richter (thomas.richter@berlin-city.de)

---

## Annual Events

### State of the City Address
**Schedule:** January (second Tuesday) at 18:00
**Location:** City Hall, Council Chamber
**Department:** Mayor's Office
**Type:** Public Address

Annual address by the Mayor reviewing the past year and outlining priorities for the coming year. Followed by reception. Livestreamed at berlin-city.de/live.

---

### Budget Public Hearing
**Schedule:** March (first Tuesday) at 18:00
**Location:** City Hall, Main Chamber
**Department:** Finance
**Type:** Public Hearing

Annual public hearing on the proposed budget for the coming fiscal year. Public testimony accepted. Written comments accepted through one week after hearing.

**Contact:** Dr. Sandra Friedrich (sandra.friedrich@berlin-city.de)

---

### Earth Day Festival
**Schedule:** April 22 (or nearest Saturday), 10:00 - 16:00
**Location:** Riverside Park
**Department:** Parks and Recreation
**Type:** Community Event

Annual celebration of Earth Day with environmental exhibits, sustainable living demonstrations, and nature activities. Free admission.

---

### Memorial Day Ceremony
**Schedule:** Last Monday of May at 10:00
**Location:** Veterans Memorial Park
**Department:** Mayor's Office
**Type:** Commemorative Event

Annual ceremony honoring fallen service members. Guest speakers, wreath laying, and moment of silence.
