# City Services Overview

## Online Services

### City Service Portal
The Berlin City Service Portal (berlin-city.de/portal) provides 24/7 access to many city services. Create a free account to access personalized services, track applications, and receive notifications.

**Available Online Services:**
- Pay property taxes, utility bills, and business licenses
- Apply for building permits and business licenses
- Request public records
- Report issues (potholes, streetlight outages, graffiti)
- Register for recreation programs and reserve facilities
- View zoning information and property records
- Sign up for city newsletters and alerts
- Access public meeting agendas and minutes

**Creating an Account:**
Visit berlin-city.de/portal and click "Create Account." You'll need a valid email address and will create a password. Verify your identity by answering security questions. Some services require additional verification with address confirmation.

**Support:**
For help with the portal, call the Digital Services Help Line at +49 30 2300 0100 (Mon-Fri 8:00-17:00) or email portal@berlin-city.de.

---

### Document Requests
Request copies of city documents online through the Public Records portal (berlin-city.de/records). Available documents include:

- Birth, death, and marriage certificates
- Property records and tax information
- Building permits and inspection reports
- Business licenses
- City maps and GIS data
- Meeting minutes and agendas
- City ordinances and resolutions

**How to Request:**
1. Visit berlin-city.de/records
2. Log in or create an account
3. Describe the document(s) you need
4. Submit request (no fee for request submission)
5. Receive cost estimate (if any) for document production
6. Approve costs and pay online
7. Documents delivered electronically or by mail

**Processing Time:** Most requests processed within 5 business days. Complex requests may take up to 10 business days. Expedited service available for additional fee.

**Contact:** Lars Schmidt, Public Records Officer (lars.schmidt@berlin-city.de, +49 30 2307 1110)

---

### E-Payments
Pay bills and fees online through the secure payment portal at berlin-city.de/payments.

**Accepted Payments:**
- Property taxes (quarterly or annual)
- Utility bills (water, electricity, waste management)
- Business licenses and permits
- Building permit fees
- Recreation program fees
- Parking tickets
- Rental assistance applications

**Payment Methods:** Credit card, debit card, or electronic bank transfer. Service fee of €1.50 applies to credit card payments. No fee for bank transfers.

**Payment Confirmation:** Immediate confirmation email and receipt. Payments processed within 1-2 business days. Allow 3-5 days for payment to appear on your account.

**Hours:** Monday-Friday 8:30-16:30
**Phone:** +49 30 2300 0000

**Services Available:**
- City Clerk's office (vital records, notary services)
- Mayor's office (appointments available)
- City Council offices
- Public Records requests
- General information desk
- Meeting room reservations

**Getting There:**
- **By transit:** Bus lines 12, 45, 67; Tram lines 1, 3
- **By car:** Public parking garage on Nebenstraße (2-hour validation available)

**Accessibility:** Building is fully accessible with elevator, accessible restrooms, and reserved parking. Assistive listening devices available for meetings. Sign language interpretation available with 48-hour notice.

## Translation and Accessibility Services

### Language Assistance
Translation services available for residents who need assistance in languages other than German. Common languages supported:
- English
- Turkish
- Polish
- Arabic
- Russian
- Vietnamese

**Request interpreter:** Call 48 hours before appointment (+49 30 2300 0311)

### Accessibility Accommodations
The city provides reasonable accommodations for residents with disabilities:
- Sign language interpretation (48-hour notice requested)
- Document translation to Braille
- Large print materials
- Assistive listening devices
- Wheelchair-accessible facilities
- Home visits for mobility-limited residents

**Request accommodations:** Contact the specific department you'll be working with at least 48 hours before your appointment, or call the City Information Line at +49 30 2300 0311.
